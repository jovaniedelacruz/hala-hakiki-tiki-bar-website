Monthly Luau

Dinner & Hula Show

Monthly Luaus take place on the last saturday evening of every month. Enjoy a dance performance by The Barefoot Hawaiian and a catered luau inspired meal. We'll be offering table service for drinks troughout the evening. Tickets only include the dinner and show.


Drinks are not included with ticket purchase.


After the show, the lounge will be business as usual.


Must be 21 and over with valid ID to attend.

Lowbrow Aloha

Lowbrow & Tiki Art Show

Join us for an afternoon of drinks and shopping at our pop-up shop featuring lowbrow & tiki goods from the finest creative talents in the Midwest. Find merchandise from idols, to shrunken heads, hawaiian shirts, commemortive mugs, paintings, pins, and more! If you love the tiki bar motif this event is a must.


The Lowbrow Aloha takes place up to three times a year so follow us on Facebook or join our newsletter to keep up with the events at Hala Kahiki!


Underage guests are permitted to attend this art show but you must be 21 and over with valid ID to consume alcohol.
