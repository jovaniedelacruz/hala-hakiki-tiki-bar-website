Reservations

To place a reservation please call during our business hours.


(708) 456-3222


Reservations must be made at least 48 hours in advance. You may call ahead thirty minutes before your arrival to be put onto a waitlist. Being added onto the waitlist is not a reservation and does not gaurantee seating. We don't accept any reservations for after 8pm on Friday and Saturday evenings.


Seating on our heated patio can be requested in your reservation.


For reservations of groups over 12 people please email us.

Large Groups & Parties

We are maintaining limited large group options but may be able to accomodate your group. Please send us an email to discuss your plans.


Have your next event at Hala Kahiki!

We have a fantastic, versatile space that can accomodate parties up to 100 people. Our table setup can be re-arranged, added to, or removed to fit your event. We do not offer food on site but can accomodate to caterers and other food provisions. Additionally, we have a large outdoor garden area available weather permitting.


We've hosted bachelor & bachellorette parties, bridal showers, baby showers, birthday parties, and wedding receptions. We can accomodate for nearly any group event.


Email us to discuss your plans: reservations@chicagotikibar.com


Attendees must be 21 years of age or older with valid state ID.

Luau Shows

Monthly Luau Shows are back! Shows take place on the last Saturday of every month. Tickets must be purchased in advance and are only available online. Seats are limited so keep up with shows dates on the events calendar or in the newsletter.
